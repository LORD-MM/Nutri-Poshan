1) Nutri-Poshan is a web-based nutrient level tracker and analyser, which aims to make mid-day meal distribution more efficient and reliable.

2) We will be providing this service to Government Schools. They can select the weekly meal plan being provided.
Data regarding distribution of these mid-day meals will be uploaded class-wise by the schools for every student consuming the meal.

3) Acccording to this data, the relevant analysis of the observed nutrient levels will be compared to that of optimal nutrient levels.
The admin will provide us data on a weekly basis and a monthly report will be generated comprising of reviews and suggested improvements.

4) This report will facilitate the schools to ensure the inclusion of proper nutrient levels in the mid-day meals on a regular basis.
