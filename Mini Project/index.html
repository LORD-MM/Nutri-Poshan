​Why there's a need for Nutri-Poshan?

​With respect to Maharashtra 6 lakh+ children are having deficit nutrition, the state has been observing a rise in the no of malnutritious children, more of which studying at government and aided schools are highly affected by the mid day meals being provided. So nutri poshan will map the void between expected and actual nutrient levels being served to the students.

​What will Nutri-Poshan do?

​It will take sectionwise weekly data of the meals being provided to the students and generate a monthly report for the same. This report in turn helps the moderator to implement better modifications to the meal plan
